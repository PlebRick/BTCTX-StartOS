BitcoinTX is a self-hosted Bitcoin portfolio tracker designed for privacy-conscious users who want full control over their financial data.

## Key Features

- **Double-Entry Accounting**: Every transaction is recorded with proper debits and credits for accurate bookkeeping.

- **FIFO Cost Basis**: Automatically calculates your cost basis using First In, First Out methodology.

- **Tax Reporting**: Generates IRS-compliant Form 8949 and Schedule D for capital gains reporting.

- **Local Storage**: All data stays on your server in a SQLite database. No cloud services or third-party access.

## Use Cases

- Track Bitcoin purchases and sales across multiple exchanges
- Calculate capital gains for tax purposes
- Maintain a complete history of your Bitcoin transactions
- Generate tax documents for annual filing

## Privacy

BitcoinTX runs entirely on your StartOS server. Your transaction data, wallet information, and tax reports are never transmitted to external services.
